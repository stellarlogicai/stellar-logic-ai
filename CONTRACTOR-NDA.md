## 3. DEFINITIONS

**3.1 "Confidential Information"** means any and all non-public information, whether or not reduced to writing, relating to the Business, including but not limited to:

(a) Technical Information: AI algorithms, detection methods, source code, software architecture, system designs, research data, and security protocols;

(b) Business Information: Customer lists, pricing structures, marketing strategies, business plans, financial data, and operational metrics;

(c) Product Information: Product roadmaps, feature specifications, performance metrics, and integration methods;

(d) Customer Information: Customer names, contact information, usage data, and security incidents;

(e) Any information marked or identified by Company as "confidential" or "proprietary."

**3.2 "Trade Secrets"** means information, regardless of form, that derives independent economic value from not being generally known to or readily ascertainable by other persons who can obtain economic value from its disclosure or use.

---

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Protection of Confidential Information.** Contractor agrees to:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;

(b) Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of providing services to Company;

(c) Not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without Company's prior written consent;

(d) Implement reasonable security measures to protect Confidential Information;

(e) Not use Confidential Information for any purpose other than providing services to Company.

**4.2 Employee Restrictions.** Contractor shall ensure that its employees, subcontractors, and agents are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those contained in this Agreement.

**4.3 Return of Information.** Upon completion of services or termination of this Agreement, Contractor shall promptly return all Confidential Information to Company.

---

## 5. NON-COMPETITION AND NON-SOLICITATION

**5.1 Non-Competition.** During the term of this Agreement and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Contractor shall not:

(a) Develop or provide competing AI security or governance services;

(b) Use any Confidential Information to create competing products or services;

(c) Assist any competitor in developing similar AI security solutions.

**5.2 Non-Solicitation.** During the term of this Agreement and for twelve (12) months following termination, Contractor shall not:

(a) Solicit any employee or customer of Company;

(b) Induce any employee or customer to terminate their relationship with Company;

(c) Hire any employee of Company who terminates employment within six (6) months.

---

## 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**6.1 Company IP Ownership.** Contractor acknowledges that all Intellectual Property created, developed, or contributed to during the provision of services shall be the sole and exclusive property of Company.

**6.2 Assignment of Rights.** Contractor hereby assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in any Intellectual Property created during the provision of services.

---

## 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

**7.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue throughout the provision of services.

**7.2 Termination.** Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice.

**7.3 Survival.** The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for a period of three (3) years.

---

## 8. REMEDIES

**8.1 Injunctive Relief.** Contractor acknowledges that any breach may cause irreparable harm and that Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief.

**8.2 Attorney's Fees.** The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.
