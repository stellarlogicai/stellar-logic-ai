## 3. DEFINITIONS

**3.1 "Confidential Information"** means any and all non-public information, whether or not reduced to writing, disclosed by either party, including but not limited to:

(a) **Helm AI Confidential Information:** AI algorithms, detection methods, source code, software architecture, system designs, research data, customer lists, pricing information, business strategies, and financial data;

(b) **Partner Confidential Information:** Business plans, customer lists, technical architectures, integration requirements, financial data, marketing strategies, and proprietary information;

(c) **Mutual Confidential Information:** Information shared between the parties during negotiations or partnership discussions;

(d) Any information marked or identified as "confidential" or "proprietary" by either party.

**3.2 "Trade Secrets"** means information, regardless of form, that derives independent economic value from not being generally known to or readily ascertainable by other persons who can obtain economic value from its disclosure or use, and is the subject of efforts that are reasonable under the circumstances to maintain its secrecy.

---

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Protection of Information.** Each party agrees to:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information received from the other party in strict confidence;

(b) Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating and implementing the potential business relationship;

(c) Not disclose any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party;

(d) Limit access to Confidential Information to employees with a "need-to-know" basis;

(e) Implement reasonable security measures to protect Confidential Information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

**4.2 Employee Restrictions.** Each party agrees to ensure that its employees, contractors, and agents are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those contained in this Agreement.

**4.3 Return of Information.** Upon termination of discussions or relationship, each party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information received from the other party, including all copies, extracts, and derivatives thereof.

---

## 5. NON-DISCLOSURE

**5.1 Permitted Disclosures.** Either party may disclose Confidential Information to:

(a) Its legal counsel, accountants, or other professional advisors;

(b) Government agencies when required by law, regulation, or court order;

(c) Potential investors or financing sources under confidentiality agreements;

(d) Insurance providers for coverage purposes;

(e) Auditors or compliance officers under appropriate circumstances;

(f) As otherwise required by applicable law or regulation.

**5.2 Notice Requirement.** When disclosure is required under Section 5.1, the disclosing party shall:

(a) Provide prior written notice to the other party when practicable;

(b) Limit disclosure to the minimum necessary to comply with the requirement;

(c) Use reasonable efforts to obtain confidential treatment or protective order;

(d) Cooperate with the other party in seeking protective measures.

---

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

**6.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for the duration of the parties' business discussions and negotiations.

**6.2 Termination.** Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time upon written notice to the other party.

**6.3 Return of Information.** Upon termination, each party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information received from the other party.

**6.4 Survival.** The confidentiality obligations set forth in this Agreement shall survive termination for a period of three (3) years thereafter.

---

## 7. NO LICENSE OR RIGHTS GRANTED

**7.1 No License.** Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as granting any license or right to use any Confidential Information.

**7.2 No Warranty.** No warranty is made by either party regarding the accuracy or completeness of any Confidential Information.

**7.3 No Obligation.** Neither party has any obligation to enter into any business relationship as a result of this Agreement.

---

## 8. REMEDIES

**8.2 Monetary Damages.** The breaching party shall be liable for all damages resulting from the breach, including reasonable attorneys' fees.
