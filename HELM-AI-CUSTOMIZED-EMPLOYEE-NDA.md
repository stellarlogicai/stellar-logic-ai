## 3. DEFINITIONS

**3.1 "Confidential Information"** means any and all non-public information, whether or not reduced to writing, relating to the Business, including but not limited to:

(a) **Technical Information:** 
   - Multi-modal AI algorithms and detection methodologies
   - Computer vision and audio analysis techniques
   - Network traffic monitoring and analysis protocols
   - Source code, software architecture, and system designs
   - Research data, testing methodologies, and security protocols
   - AI model training data and optimization techniques
   - False positive rates and accuracy metrics
   - Integration APIs and SDK documentation
   - Security vulnerability assessments and remediation strategies

(b) **Business Information:**
   - Customer lists including gaming studios and enterprise clients
   - Pricing structures, revenue models, and financial data
   - Marketing strategies, business plans, and operational metrics
   - Partnership agreements and strategic alliance details
   - Investor information and funding strategies
   - Market analysis and competitive intelligence

(c) **Product Information:**
   - Product roadmaps, feature specifications, and development timelines
   - Performance metrics, accuracy rates, and benchmarking data
   - Customer integration details and configuration settings
   - Security incident reports and resolution procedures
   - Competitive analysis and market positioning

(d) **Customer Information:**
   - Gaming studio names, contact information, and usage data
   - Enterprise client details, integration requirements, and performance data
   - Security incident logs, detection statistics, and compliance reports
   - Customer feedback, testimonials, and case study details

(e) **Employee Information:**
   - Employee lists, compensation data, and performance reviews
   - Internal communications, meeting minutes, and strategic decisions
   - Hiring plans, organizational structure, and management information

(f) **Any information** marked or identified by Company as "confidential," "proprietary," or "trade secret."

**3.2 "Trade Secrets"** means information, regardless of form, that derives independent economic value from not being generally known to or readily ascertainable by other persons who can obtain economic value from its disclosure or use, and is the subject of efforts that are reasonable under the circumstances to maintain its secrecy, including but not limited to:

- Multi-modal AI detection algorithms and methodologies
- Computer vision and audio analysis techniques
- Network traffic analysis protocols
- Customer integration methods and configurations
- Security vulnerability detection and prevention methods
- Performance optimization techniques and accuracy metrics

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Protection of Confidential Information.** Employee agrees to:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence and take all reasonable precautions to protect such Confidential Information from unauthorized disclosure;

(b) Use Confidential Information solely for the benefit of Company and not for any personal gain or for the benefit of any other person or entity;

(c) Not disclose, publish, or communicate any Confidential Information to any third party without Company's prior written consent;

(d) Not copy, reproduce, or reverse engineer any Confidential Information except as necessary for performance of job duties;

(e) Not use any Confidential Information for any purpose other than the performance of Employee's duties for Company;

(f) Implement and maintain appropriate security measures to protect Confidential Information;

(g) Report any suspected security breaches or unauthorized disclosures immediately.

**4.2 Return of Company Property.** Upon termination of employment, Employee shall promptly return to Company all documents, materials, equipment, and other property containing Confidential Information, including all copies, extracts, and derivatives thereof.

**4.3 Continued Obligations.** The obligations set forth in this Agreement shall survive the termination of Employee's employment and shall continue for a period of five (5) years thereafter.

---

## 5. NON-COMPETITION

**5.1 Non-Competition.** During Employee's employment and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee shall not:

(a) Directly or indirectly engage in any business that competes with Company's Business, including but not limited to:
   - AI anti-cheat systems for gaming platforms
   - AI governance platforms for enterprise use
   - Multi-modal AI security solutions
   - Computer vision-based security systems
   - Network traffic analysis for security purposes

(b) Work for any competitor of Company in any capacity that involves the use or disclosure of Confidential Information;

(c) Solicit or attempt to solicit any employee or customer of Company to terminate their relationship with Company;

(d) Provide services to any person or entity that is engaged in developing AI security or governance solutions;

(e) Use any Confidential Information to develop competing products or services.

**5.2 Non-Solicitation.** During Employee's employment and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, Employee shall not:

(a) Directly or indirectly solicit for employment any person who is an employee of Company;

(b) Induce or attempt to induce any employee of Company to terminate their employment;

(c) Hire any employee of Company who terminates employment within six (6) months after Employee's termination;

(d) Solicit any customer or partner of Company for competing services.

## 7. RETURN OF INFORMATION

**7.1 Immediate Return.** Upon termination of employment for any reason, Employee shall immediately return to Company all documents, materials, equipment, and other property containing Confidential Information.

**7.2 No Retention.** Employee shall not retain any copies, extracts, or derivatives of any Confidential Information after termination of employment.

**7.3 Certification.** Employee shall provide a written certification to Company confirming that all Confidential Information has been returned and no copies have been retained.

## 8. REMEDIES

**8.1 Injunctive Relief.** Employee acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to Company and that monetary damages alone would be an inadequate remedy. Accordingly, Employee agrees that Company shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief to prevent or stop any breach of this Agreement.

**8.2 Enhanced Damages.** In addition to any other remedies available, Employee shall be liable for:

(a) All profits, benefits, or advantages obtained from any breach;

(b) The value of any trade secrets misappropriated;

(c) The cost of developing replacement technology;

(d) All reasonable attorney's fees and costs;

(e) Any other damages resulting from the breach.

**8.3 Attorney's Fees.** In the event of any action or proceeding to enforce this Agreement, the prevailing party shall be entitled to recover its reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.
