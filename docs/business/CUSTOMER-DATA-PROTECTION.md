## 2. RECITALS

WHEREAS, Customer will be using Helm AI's AI Anti-Cheat System and/or AI Governance Platform (the "Services");

WHEREAS, Customer may provide certain data to Helm AI for the purpose of receiving the Services;

WHEREAS, Helm AI will process and analyze such data as part of providing the Services;

WHEREAS, Both parties wish to ensure adequate protection of Customer's data and compliance with applicable data protection laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**3.1 "Customer Data"** means any data, information, or materials provided by Customer to Helm AI in connection with the Services, including but not limited to:

(a) **Gaming Data:** Player behavior data, game logs, network traffic, user interactions, and gaming platform data;

(b) **AI Governance Data:** AI model outputs, governance metrics, compliance data, and enterprise AI usage data;

(c) **Security Data:** Security incident reports, vulnerability assessments, threat intelligence, and security event logs;

(d) **Personal Data:** Any personal information, personal data, or personally identifiable information subject to data protection laws;

(e) **Performance Data:** System performance metrics, accuracy statistics, and service usage data;

(f) **Configuration Data:** Integration settings, system configurations, and deployment parameters.

**3.2 "Data Processing"** means any operation performed on Customer Data, including collection, storage, use, analysis, transmission, and deletion.

**3.3 "Data Subject"** means an individual who is the subject of Personal Data.

**3.4 "Data Protection Laws"** means all applicable data protection and privacy laws, including GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and other relevant regulations.

---

## 4. DATA PROTECTION OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Data Security.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Customer Data;

(b) Use encryption for all Customer Data at rest and in transit;

(c) Implement access controls and authentication mechanisms;

(d) Conduct regular security assessments and penetration testing;

(e) Maintain security incident response procedures;

(f) Comply with industry security standards and best practices.

**4.2 Data Minimization.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Process only the Customer Data necessary to provide the Services;

(b) Not use Customer Data for any purpose other than providing the Services;

(c) Retain Customer Data only as long as necessary for the Services;

(d) Delete Customer Data upon Customer's request or termination of Services;

(e) Provide Customer with data deletion and retention options.

**4.3 Data Processing Limitations.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Process Customer Data only in accordance with Customer's instructions;

(b) Not sell, license, or otherwise monetize Customer Data;

(c) Not combine Customer Data with data from other customers;

(d) Not use Customer Data to train AI models without explicit consent;

(e) Not disclose Customer Data to third parties without Customer's consent.

---

## 5. GDPR COMPLIANCE

**5.1 Data Processor Role.** Helm AI acknowledges that it acts as a Data Processor on behalf of Customer for purposes of GDPR.

**5.2 GDPR Obligations.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Customer;

(b) Ensure that persons authorized to process Personal Data are committed to confidentiality;

(c) Take appropriate security measures to protect Personal Data;

(d) Assist Customer in responding to data subject rights requests;

(e) Assist Customer in conducting data protection impact assessments;

(f) Notify Customer of any personal data breach without undue delay;

(g) Delete or return all Personal Data upon termination of services;

(h) Make available to Customer all information necessary to demonstrate compliance.

**5.3 Data Subject Rights.** Helm AI shall assist Customer in fulfilling data subject rights, including:

(a) Right to access personal data;

(b) Right to rectification of personal data;

(c) Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten");

(d) Right to restriction of processing;

(e) Right to data portability;

(f) Right to object to processing;

(g) Rights related to automated decision making and profiling.

---

## 6. CCPA COMPLIANCE

**6.1 CCPA Obligations.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Treat Customer Data as "personal information" under CCPA;

(b) Not sell Customer's personal information without explicit consent;

(c) Honor Customer's instructions regarding personal information rights;

(d) Provide reasonable security measures to protect personal information;

(e) Assist Customer in responding to consumer requests;

(f) Maintain records of personal information processing activities.

**6.2 Consumer Rights.** Helm AI shall assist Customer in fulfilling consumer rights under CCPA, including:

(a) Right to know what personal information is collected;

(b) Right to delete personal information;

(c) Right to opt-out of sale of personal information;

(d) Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

---

## 7. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

**7.1 Notification Requirements.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Notify Customer without undue delay after becoming aware of any personal data breach;

(b) Provide sufficient information to enable Customer to meet its notification obligations;

(c) Include details of the breach, categories of data affected, and mitigation measures;

(d) Cooperate with Customer in responding to the breach;

(e) Provide reasonable assistance in investigating and remediating the breach.

**7.2 Notification Content.** The breach notification shall include:

(a) Nature of the personal data breach;

(b) Categories and approximate number of data subjects affected;

(c) Categories and approximate number of personal data records concerned;

(d) Likely consequences of the personal data breach;

(e) Measures taken or proposed to address the breach;

(f) Measures recommended to mitigate potential adverse effects.

---

## 8. DATA TRANSFER AND STORAGE

**8.1 Data Transfer.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Transfer Customer Data only to jurisdictions with adequate data protection;

(b) Use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers;

(c) Obtain Customer's consent for any data transfers outside the agreed jurisdictions;

(d) Maintain records of all data transfers.

**8.2 Data Storage.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Store Customer Data in secure, access-controlled environments;

(b) Use encrypted storage for all Customer Data;

(c) Maintain geographic segregation of Customer Data as required;

(d) Provide Customer with information about data storage locations.

---

## 9. AUDIT AND COMPLIANCE

**9.1 Right to Audit.** Customer shall have the right to audit Helm AI's compliance with this Addendum, provided that:

(a) Reasonable notice is given to Helm AI;

(b) Audit is conducted during normal business hours;

(c) Audit does not unreasonably interfere with Helm AI's operations;

(d) Confidential information of other customers is protected.

**9.2 Compliance Certifications.** Helm AI shall:

(a) Maintain relevant security and privacy certifications;

(b) Provide evidence of compliance with Data Protection Laws;

(c) Conduct regular compliance assessments;

(d) Make available compliance documentation to Customer.

---

## 10. TERM AND TERMINATION

**10.1 Term.** This Addendum shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for the duration of the MSA.

**10.2 Termination.** Upon termination of the MSA, Helm AI shall:

(a) Immediately stop processing Customer Data;

(b) Return or delete all Customer Data within 30 days;

(c) Provide certification of data deletion;

(d) Continue to protect any retained data as required by law.

---

## 11. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

**11.1 Data Protection Liability.** Helm AI shall be liable for any damages resulting from its breach of this Addendum.

**11.2 Indemnification.** Helm AI shall indemnify Customer against any third-party claims arising from Helm AI's breach of data protection obligations.

**11.3 Limitation of Liability.** The liability limitations in the MSA shall not apply to breaches of this Addendum.

---

**12.2 Modification.** This Addendum may be amended only by written agreement of both parties.

**12.3 Severability.** If any provision is invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.

**12.4 Governing Law.** This Addendum shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
