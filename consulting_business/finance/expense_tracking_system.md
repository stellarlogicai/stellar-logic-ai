## **DAILY EXPENSE MANAGEMENT**

### **Receipt Capture Process**
**Immediate Action Required:**
- **Take photo** of every receipt immediately
- **Categorize** expense within 24 hours
- **Add notes** about business purpose
- **Tag client/project** if applicable
- **Verify amount** and vendor information
- **Store receipt** in organized digital folder

**Mobile App Workflow:**
1. Open expense tracking app
2. Take photo of receipt
3. Enter amount and vendor
4. Select expense category
5. Add business purpose notes
6. Save and categorize
7. Archive physical receipt (if needed)

## **TAX-DEDUCTIBLE EXPENSE GUIDELINES**

### **Common Business Deductions**
**Fully Deductible:**
- Software and technology subscriptions
- Business insurance premiums
- Professional development and training
- Marketing and advertising costs
- Office supplies and equipment
- Legal and professional services

**Partially Deductible:**
- Home office expenses (business portion)
- Vehicle expenses (business mileage)
- Meals and entertainment (50% deductible)
- Travel expenses (business portion)
- Health insurance premiums (self-employed deduction)

---

### **Documentation Requirements**
**IRS Requirements:**
- **Amount:** Exact cost of each expense
- **Time:** Date of purchase or service
- **Place:** Location where expense occurred
- **Business Purpose:** How expense relates to business
- **Supporting Documents:** Receipts, invoices, contracts

**Record Retention:**
- **3 years** for standard tax returns
- **7 years** for business asset depreciation
- **Digital storage** acceptable for IRS
- **Backup systems** required for data security
- **Organized by year** and expense category
