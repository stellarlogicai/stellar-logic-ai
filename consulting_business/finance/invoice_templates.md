## **INVOICE CREATION INSTRUCTIONS**

### **PayPal Invoice Creation:**
1. **Login to PayPal Business** account
2. **Go to Invoicing** section
3. **Click "Create Invoice"**
4. **Fill in client information**
5. **Add service items** with descriptions and amounts
6. **Set payment terms** (Net 15 days)
7. **Send invoice** to client email
8. **Copy invoice link** for your records

### **Customization Tips:**
- **Add your logo** in PayPal settings
- **Use professional descriptions** for services
- **Include detailed breakdown** of work performed
- **Set up automatic reminders** for late payments
- **Enable partial payments** for 50/50 split

### **Invoice Numbering:**
- **SL-2024-001** for AI Security Audit
- **ML-2024-001** for Custom ML Development
