# Client Discovery Framework

## Overview
**Purpose:** Systematic approach to assess client AI security readiness and needs, enabling targeted service recommendations and improved project outcomes.

---

## Client Readiness Assessment

### Phase 1: Initial Discovery (First Contact)
**Objective:** Understand client's current AI security posture and identify immediate needs.

**Key Questions:**
1. What AI/ML systems are currently deployed?
2. What are the primary use cases and user volumes?
3. What security measures are currently in place?
4. Have you experienced any AI-related security incidents?
5. What's your current security budget and timeline?
6. Who are the key decision-makers for security initiatives?

**Assessment Tools:**
- Readiness questionnaire (initial)
- Security measures inventory
- Staff expertise evaluation
- Budget and timeline assessment
- Risk tolerance analysis

**Success Criteria:**
- Client articulates current security challenges clearly
- Decision-maker identified and engaged
- Initial assessment completed within 30 minutes
- Readiness score established (baseline)

---

### Phase 2: Technical Deep Dive (Follow-up)
**Objective:** Comprehensive analysis of client's AI systems and security architecture.

**Technical Assessment Areas:**
1. **System Inventory**
   - AI/ML models in production
   - Data processing pipelines
   - Integration points and dependencies
   - System owners and maintenance responsibilities

2. **Security Architecture Review**
   - Current security controls and measures
   - Access control and authentication systems
   - Monitoring and logging capabilities
   - Network segmentation and isolation

3. **Threat Modeling**
   - Potential attack vectors and threat actors
   - Vulnerability assessment methodology
   - Risk scoring and prioritization matrix
   - Incident response procedures

**Assessment Tools:**
- System inventory templates
- Security architecture review checklists
- Threat modeling frameworks
- Vulnerability scanning tools
- Risk assessment matrices

**Success Criteria:**
- Comprehensive technical understanding of client environment
- Security gaps and vulnerabilities identified
- Technical recommendations prioritized by risk level
- Client understands technical findings and implications
- Detailed technical report delivered

---

### Phase 3: Business Context Analysis
**Objective:** Understand client's business context, regulatory environment, and strategic goals.

**Business Analysis Areas:**
1. **Industry and Regulatory Environment**
   - Industry-specific compliance requirements
   - Competitive landscape and positioning
   - Regulatory constraints and obligations
   - Market trends and emerging threats

2. **Organizational Factors**
   - Company culture and change management
   - Budget cycles and approval processes
   - Stakeholder mapping and influence analysis
   - Risk appetite and tolerance levels

3. **Strategic Alignment**
   - Business goals and AI strategy alignment
   - Growth plans and scalability requirements
   - Success metrics and KPI definitions
   - Resource constraints and limitations

**Assessment Tools:**
- Business context questionnaires
- Stakeholder mapping templates
- Regulatory requirement checklists
- Competitive analysis frameworks
- Strategic alignment matrices

**Success Criteria:**
- Business context thoroughly understood and documented
- Strategic alignment between security initiatives and business goals achieved
- Regulatory requirements identified and addressed
- Stakeholder buy-in secured with clear business case

## Readiness Scoring System

### Scoring Matrix
**High Readiness (8-10 points):**
- Clear AI strategy with defined goals (2)
- Comprehensive security measures implemented (2)
- Dedicated security budget allocated (2)
- Staff with AI security expertise (1)
- Proactive security posture (1)
- Executive support for security (1)
- Compliance framework established (1)

**Medium Readiness (4-7 points):**
- AI strategy in development (1)
- Some security measures implemented (1)
- Security budget consideration (1)
- Partial stakeholder alignment (1)
- Reactive security posture (0)

**Low Readiness (0-3 points):**
- No AI strategy or security plan (0)
- Ad-hoc security approach (0)
- No dedicated security resources (0)
- Low security awareness (0)
- Reactive security posture (0)
- No executive support (0)

### Scoring Criteria
**Technical Factors (40%):**
- AI systems inventory completeness
- Security architecture maturity
- Vulnerability assessment capability
- Monitoring and logging sophistication

**Business Factors (30%):**
- Industry and regulatory compliance
- Strategic alignment with business goals
- Stakeholder engagement and buy-in
- Budget allocation for security initiatives
- Risk management processes

**Organizational Factors (30%):**
- Executive support and sponsorship
- Staff training and expertise
- Change management capability
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Security culture and awareness

## Implementation Process

### Step 1: Assessment Execution
1. **Pre-Call Research** - Review company background, recent activities
2. **Assessment Planning** - Prepare questions and tools
3. **Discovery Session** - Conduct structured assessment
4. **Scoring & Analysis** - Calculate readiness score
5. **Reporting** - Deliver initial findings and recommendations

### Step 2: Service Recommendation
1. **Gap Analysis** - Map assessment findings to service offerings
2. **Solution Design** - Create tailored service package
3. **Business Case Development** - Build ROI justification
4. **Proposal Generation** - Create detailed service proposal
5. **Presentation** - Deliver findings and recommendations

### Step 3: Follow-up and Refinement
1. **Client Feedback** - Gather response to assessment and proposals
2. **Service Refinement** - Adjust offerings based on feedback
3. **Implementation Planning** - Create detailed project roadmap
4. **Ongoing Support** - Continuous improvement and optimization

2. **Technical Deep Dive** - Use after initial interest is confirmed

4. **Ongoing** - Use for continuous client relationship management
