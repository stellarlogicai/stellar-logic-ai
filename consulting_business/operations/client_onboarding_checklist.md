# STELLAR LOGIC AI - CLIENT ONBOARDING CHECKLIST

## **PHASE 1: INITIAL CONTACT**

### **Discovery Call Preparation**
- [ ] Research client company and industry
- [ ] Review client's initial inquiry/reason for contact
- [ ] Prepare discovery call questions
- [ ] Test video/audio equipment
- [ ] Confirm meeting time and access details

### **Discovery Call Execution**
- [ ] Introduce Stellar Logic AI and services
- [ ] Understand client's AI security needs and challenges
- [ ] Ask about current AI systems and security measures
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders and decision-makers
- [ ] Discuss timeline and budget expectations
- [ ] Document all client requirements and concerns

### **Post-Call Follow-up**
- [ ] Send thank you email with call summary
- [ ] Include key discussion points and next steps
- [ ] Attach initial proposal outline
- [ ] Schedule follow-up call if needed
- [ ] Update CRM with contact information

- [ ] Schedule project kickoff meeting
