Our Story

Founded in 2024, Stellar Logic AI emerged from a critical need in the enterprise landscape: as artificial intelligence systems became increasingly sophisticated and integral to business operations, the security challenges surrounding these systems grew exponentially.

Our founders, veterans in both cybersecurity and machine learning, recognized that traditional security approaches were insufficient for the unique vulnerabilities and threats facing AI systems. They set out to create a consulting firm that would bridge this gap.

Today, Stellar Logic AI provides expert AI security consulting, helping organizations across industries protect their AI investments, ensure compliance, and maintain trust in their artificial intelligence systems.
