## **CASE STUDY CREATION GUIDELINES**

### **Structure Requirements**
- **Executive Summary:** One-paragraph overview of challenge and solution
- **Client Background:** Brief company context and industry position
- **Problem Statement:** Clear articulation of the business challenge
- **Solution Approach:** Detailed methodology and process
- **Implementation:** Specific actions and timeline
- **Results:** Quantifiable outcomes and business impact
- **Client Quote:** Direct testimonial from client contact

### **Writing Best Practices**
- **Use Metrics:** Include specific numbers and percentages
- **Focus on Impact:** Emphasize business outcomes and ROI
- **Be Specific:** Use concrete examples and technical details
- **Client Voice:** Maintain professional yet approachable tone
- **Problem-Solution:** Clear connection between challenge and resolution

### **Approval Process**
- **Client Review:** Send draft to client for accuracy approval
- **Confidentiality:** Ensure sensitive information is protected
- **Branding:** Include Stellar Logic AI branding consistently
- **Format:** Professional layout with clear headings
- **Contact Info:** Include client contact information
