# STELLAR LOGI AI - CLIENT REFERRAL PROGRAM

## **INCENTIVE SYSTEM FOR NEW BUSINESS ACQUISITION**

### **Referral Program Overview**
- **Purpose:** Leverage satisfied clients to generate new business leads
- **Structure:** Tiered rewards for successful referrals
- **Target:** 20% of new business from client referrals
- **Timeline:** Ongoing program with quarterly reviews
- **Budget:** 10% of first project value allocated to referral rewards

---

## **REFERRAL PROGRAM STRUCTURE**

### **Referral Tiers and Rewards**
**Tier 1: Qualified Referral**
- **Requirement:** Referral leads to initial consultation
- **Reward:** $100 Amazon gift card
- **Timeline:** Paid within 30 days of consultation
- **Tracking:** Referral code system for attribution

**Tier 2: Converted Referral**
- **Requirement:** Referral becomes paying client
- **Reward:** 10% of first project value (up to $1,000)
- **Timeline:** Paid upon client first payment
- **Bonus:** Additional $200 if project value exceeds $10,000

**Tier 3: Enterprise Referral**
- **Requirement:** Referral signs annual contract ($50,000+)
- **Reward:** 15% of first year contract value (up to $5,000)
- **Timeline:** Paid upon client first payment
- **Bonus:** Free AI security audit for referring client

**Tier 4: Multiple Referrals**
- **Requirement:** 3+ successful referrals in 12 months
- **Reward:** 20% bonus on all referral earnings
- **Additional:** Premium LinkedIn membership renewal
- **Recognition:** Featured in client success stories

---

### **Referral Process Workflow**
**Step 1: Referral Submission**
- Client submits referral through online form
- Includes referral contact information and project details
- Referral code automatically generated
- Confirmation email sent to referring client

**Step 2: Referral Qualification**
- Stellar Logic AI team contacts referral within 48 hours
- Initial consultation scheduled within 1 week
- Referral status updated in client portal
- Referring client notified of progress

**Step 3: Conversion Tracking**
- Referral progress tracked in CRM system
- Automated status updates sent to referrer
- Project milestones communicated
- Reward eligibility confirmed

**Step 4: Reward Fulfillment**
- Reward calculation based on conversion tier
- Reward payment processed within 30 days
- Tax documentation provided (Form 1099)
- Public recognition (with permission)
