Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with Helm AI?

Sign up for an account, choose your plan, and follow our quick start guide. You'll be up and running in minutes with our SDK and API.

What programming languages are supported?

We support Node.js, Python, Java, C#, Go, and Ruby. Our REST API can be used with any programming language.

How accurate is the AI Anti-Cheat System?

Our AI Anti-Cheat System achieves 99.9% accuracy in detecting sophisticated cheating methods using multi-modal analysis.

Can I customize detection rules?

Yes, you can create custom detection rules, set sensitivity levels, and configure response actions through our dashboard.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, phone support for enterprise customers, and comprehensive documentation.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
